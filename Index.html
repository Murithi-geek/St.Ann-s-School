Our History

St. Ann Mission Day & Boarding Primary School was established with a vision to provide quality education rooted in Christian values. Our institution has been serving the community for decades, nurturing young minds and preparing them for a bright future. We have grown from a small mission school to a comprehensive educational institution that serves both day and boarding students.

The Mission

Our mission is to provide holistic education that develops the intellectual, spiritual, social, and physical potential of every child. We strive to create a nurturing environment where students can grow academically, morally, and spiritually, preparing them to become responsible citizens and leaders of tomorrow.

Vision

To be a leading educational institution that empowers students to excel academically, grow spiritually, and contribute positively to society. We envision a school where every child discovers their unique potential and develops the skills, knowledge, and character needed to succeed in an ever-changing world.
